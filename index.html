Does Stanford Stand For War?

A Visual Reconnaissance of the Military Industrial Complex and its Relationship to Stanford University, 2002-2023

This project is an investigation, or a visual reconnaissance, into some key elements of the global war economy since 9/11 (between 2002 and 2023). The story that this article tells connects global war, the United States, and Stanford. This article has three main parts. The first section of the article aims to illustrate the scale of the US military and offers a glimpse into the US's participation in arms exportation across the world. The second section shifts the focus to the companies which act as weapons and defense contractors, profiting from war. The final section focuses on Stanford’s relationship to these companies and the broader US military industrial complex.
