Tracing the Infrastructure of War, from Across the Globe to Stanford University: A Visual Reconnaissance of the Military Industrial Complex

This project hopes to contribute to elucidating the role of the United States and Stanford in today’s capitalist-imperialist world system, especially when it comes to war and militarization. This article has three main parts. The first section of the article aims to illustrate the scale of the US military and offers a glimpse into the US's participation in arms exportation across the world. The second section shifts the focus to the companies which act as weapons and defense contractors, profiting from war. The final section focuses on Stanford’s relationship to these companies and the broader US military industrial complex.
